Vision

Vision

NoorLocator is built on a simple but powerful idea: no follower of Ahlulbayt (AS) should feel disconnected from their community, no matter where they are in the world.

+

The platform focuses on global accessibility, location relevance, and stronger community connection across Europe and beyond.

P
Problem statement

Problem statement

The manifesto identifies recurring challenges that NoorLocator exists to solve.

Finding nearby Shia centers can be difficult when community spaces are scattered or not clearly visible online.

People often lack timely awareness of majalis, gatherings, and center activities in their area.

Language barriers make it harder for diaspora communities to access events and feel fully connected.

M
Mission

Mission

NoorLocator exists to connect, inform, and empower people through trusted community infrastructure.

Connect users to the nearest Shia centers.

Enable centers to communicate their activities and public information clearly.

Empower community contributions with moderation that protects authenticity and accuracy.

C
Core principles

Core principles

The product experience and system rules are intentionally shaped by the manifesto.

T

Trust and authenticity

Public center data is moderated because religious community information must be accurate, verified, and safe to trust.

C

Community-driven, admin-controlled

Users contribute, admins validate, and managers maintain center content so the platform can grow without misinformation.

L

Multi-language accessibility

Structured language support reflects the diversity of diaspora communities and helps non-Arabic speakers find meaningful access.

P

Location relevance

Nearest centers, accessible languages, and relevant events should appear where they matter most to the user.
