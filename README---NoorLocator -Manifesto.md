# **🌍 Noor Locator — Manifesto**

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful.

## **1\. Vision**

**NoorLocator** is built on a simple but powerful idea:  
**No follower of Ahlulbayt (AS) should feel disconnected from their community—no matter where they are in the world.**

Across Europe and beyond, thousands of Shia Muslims struggle to:

* Find nearby Islamic centers (حسينية / mosques)  
* Stay informed about majalis and religious gatherings  
* Connect with their local community in their own language

This application exists to solve that problem.

It is not just a directory.  
It is a **living, community-driven ecosystem** for connection, knowledge, and service.

**Noor Locator** is proudly initiated and driven by **موكب خدام اهل البيت (عليهم السلام)** based in Copenhagen, Denmark.

---

## **2\. Mission**

To build a **trusted, structured, and scalable platform** that:

* Connects users to the nearest Shia centers  
* Enables centers to communicate their activities  
* Empowers the community to contribute while maintaining authenticity and accuracy

---

## **3\. Core Principles**

### **🔒 Trust & Authenticity**

All data must be **verified and moderated**.  
No center is published without approval.

---

### **🤝 Community-Driven, Admin-Controlled**

Users contribute.  
Admins validate.  
Managers maintain.

This balance ensures:

* Growth without chaos  
* Participation without misinformation

---

### **🌍 Accessibility Across Languages**

Islam is universal, and so is this platform.

Every majlis and center can be associated with **multiple languages**, ensuring:

* Inclusion of diverse communities  
* Ease of access for non-Arabic speakers

---

### **📍 Relevance Through Location**

The platform prioritizes **what matters most to the user right now**:

* Nearest centers  
* Upcoming majalis  
* Events in accessible languages

---

## **4\. System Architecture**

### **👤 User Roles**

#### **1\. Guest**

* Browse centers and majalis

#### **2\. Registered User**

* Submit center requests  
* Suggest languages and improvements  
* Contribute to platform accuracy

#### **3\. Center Manager**

* Official representative of a center  
* Publishes majalis (events)  
* Maintains center-specific content

#### **4\. Admin**

* Moderates all data  
* Approves centers, managers, and suggestions  
* Maintains system integrity

---

## **5\. Core Features**

### **📍 Center Discovery**

* Automatic geolocation  
* Nearest centers displayed  
* Search by city or country

---

### **🏢 Center Profiles**

Each center contains:

* Name, location, and contact info  
* Description  
* Supported languages  
* Upcoming majalis

---

### **🕯 Majalis Management**

Managed by verified center managers:

* Title, description, date, and time  
* Associated languages  
* Linked to specific centers

---

### **🗳 Moderated Center Requests**

Users can submit new centers:

* Stored as **pending requests**  
* Reviewed and approved by admins  
* Ensures accuracy and prevents spam

---

### **🧑‍💼 Manager System**

* Users can request to manage a center  
* Admin approval required  
* Managers can only control their assigned center

---

### **🌍 Language System**

Structured and scalable:

* Predefined language database (Arabic, Swedish, English, etc.)  
* Many-to-many relationships with majalis  
* User suggestions for center languages (admin-approved)

---

### **💬 Suggestions & Feedback**

Users can:

* Suggest corrections  
* Report missing information  
* Propose new features

All feedback is tracked and reviewed.

---
